CMS ENERGY Corp. said management would recommend to its board today that its common stock dividend be reinstated at a " modest level " later this year . The Dearborn , Mich . , energy company stopped paying a dividend in the third quarter of 1984 because of troubles at its Midland nuclear plant . In addition , CMS reported third-quarter net of $ 68.2 million , or 83 cents a share , up from $ 66.8 million , or 81 cents a share , a year ago .
