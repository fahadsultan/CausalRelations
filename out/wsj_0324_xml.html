QVC Network Inc. said it completed its acquisition of CVN Cos . for about $ 423 million . QVC agreed to pay $ 19 and one-eighth QVC share for each of CVN 's 20 million fully diluted shares . The acquisition brings together the two largest competitors to Home Shopping Network Inc., which now reaches more viewers than any other company in the video shopping industry . Among them , Home Shopping , QVC and CVN already control most of that young and fast-growing market , which last year had sales of about $ 1.4 billion .
