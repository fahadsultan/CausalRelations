Waxman Industries Inc. said holders of $ 6,542,000 face amount of its 6 1/4 % convertible subordinated debentures , due March 15 , 2007 , have elected to convert the debt into about 683,000 common shares . The conversion price is $ 9.58 a share . The company said the holders represent 52 % of the face amount of the debentures . Waxman sells a variety of hardware products for the home repair market .
