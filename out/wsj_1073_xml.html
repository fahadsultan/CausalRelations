Advanced Medical Technologies Inc. said it purchased 93 % of a unit of Henley Group Inc. Advanced Medical paid $ 106 million in cash for its share in a unit of Henley 's Fisher Scientific subsidiary . The unit makes intravenous pumps used by hospitals and had more than $ 110 million in sales last year , according to Advanced Medical .
