GOODY PRODUCTS Inc. cut its quarterly dividend to five cents a share from 11.5 cents a share . The reduced dividend is payable Jan . 2 to stock of record Dec . 15 . The Kearny , N.J.-based maker of hair accessories and other cosmetic products said it cut the dividend due to its third-quarter loss of $ 992,000 , or 15 cents a share . In the year-ago quarter , the company reported net income of $ 1.9 million , or 29 cents a share . The company also adopted an anti-takeover plan .
