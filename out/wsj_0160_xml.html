Savin Corp. reported a third-quarter net loss of $ 35.2 million , or 31 cents a share , compared with year-earlier profit of $ 3.8 million , or one cent a share . " The charges were partly offset by a $ 2 million gain on the sale of investments of two joint ventures , he said . Revenue declined 8 % to $ 85.7 million , from $ 93.3 million a year earlier . Savin cited " a general softening in the demand for office products in the market segments in which Savin competes . "
