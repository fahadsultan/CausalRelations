Dime Savings Bank of New York was cleared by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. to acquire Starpointe Savings Bank of Somerset , N.J., the banks said . Starpointe holders , who approved the plan last April , will receive $ 21 in cash a share , or a total $ 63 million . The FDIC cleared the move yesterday , and the banks must wait at least 30 days before closing the purchase . A closing date has n't been set . The thrifts agreed to the transaction in August 1988 .
